Hi I'm John.
I'm an artist engaged in creating beautiful things out of the warm medium of wood. Phone pucks came about out of convenience. I like to watch things on my phone while I eat, and having to prop my phone up on various objects all the time was annoying. I needed a means of holding up my phone, and at the same time I wanted it to be a beautiful element that could remain on the table and not be out of place.

In addition to their beauty and function, phone pucks also serve me well by being small, portable, and nearly indestructible. I carry one in my laptop case for use at coffee shops, and their function has caught the attention of my friends, many of whom now have a phone puck on their own dining tables.

My mission with this online shop is to make my phone pucks available to the rest of the world in a manner I can sustain out of my home.
